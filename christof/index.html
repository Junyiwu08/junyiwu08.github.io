Background

Since 2010, the Make Up Services has been entrusted with the care of women. With the help of volunteers and partners, we invite some experts who good at make up. We share their suggestion with more than 275 million people every year. But our work doesn’t stop here.

PROBLEM/CHALLENGE

Nowadays, more and more people live in the big city. They are busy everyday, Especially, some girls don’t have enough time to enjoy make up, and also they don’t know what kind of skin care is benefit to their skin. Therefore, Chrisdof Beaute app will help them solve these problems and also save their time.

With the help of volunteers and partners, we invite some experts who good at make up. And we share their suggestion with more than 275 million people every year. We hope our team can help more and more girls. Let them become beautiful.

INTERVIEW

The report concludes with recommendations for ways to increase purchase makeup, to translate awareness into increased purchase. Providing accessible, and desirable experiences can help women to enjoy make up and learn how to make up in their life.

RESEARCH/SURVEY

The field of user experience has a wide range of research methods available. For the make up, there are many ways to learn how to do it. Also, Unmoderated online UX assessments can help people to use the application easily.

WIREFRAME

A wireframe is a visual representation of a user interface, stripped of any visual design or branding elements. It is used by UX Designers to define the heirachy of items on a screen and communicate what the items on that page should be based on user needs.

RESPONSIVE DESIGN

Responsive web design is an approach to web design which makes web pages render well on a variety of devices. Recent work also considers the viewer proximity as part of the viewing proximity as part of the viewing context as an extension for RWD.
