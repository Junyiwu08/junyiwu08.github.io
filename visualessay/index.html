Desktop Icon with Big Data

Statement: Between digital environment and physical object,  what is desktop icon? Can desktop icon related to something else, such as culture? Especially, the big data has much powerful capability for the  social and individual. Is that possible to understanding that philosophy of Big Data by using basic function of desktop icon?

Thesis:Discuss the profound influence that big data brings to people through the form of desktop icon, and the the future tendency of big data.

Conspectus:Introduce the definition of desktop icon and big data. And figure out that the function and elements of them. And discuss how make connection between desktop icon and the aspect of culture. ( Different culture has their own visual system). Introduce the thesis: how big data affects people through the form of desktop icon and the future tendency of big data? Reason: when interface designer designed the desktop icon, big data can help designer to collect the complex information about shape and color which is people’s favorite and acceptable. What’s more, discuss the basic definition of big data, go through them, know about the function of big data. Introduce the philosophy of big data (prove example: an ideology or action?) Debate: talking about the influence of big data from the different sides. How big data affect people’s life? why the big data has much powerful capability for the  social and individual? ( From positive and negative aspects; good influence and bad influences) Conclusion: Why big data is important to people? And why it has a huge influence with desktop icon’s form, what’s the future tendency of big data? Collect the people’s comment about big data.
